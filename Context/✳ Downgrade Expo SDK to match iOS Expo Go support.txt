  The one thing you do need to set on Supabase (not a secret)

  Password auth is on by default, but two gotchas:

  1. Email confirmation. If "Confirm email" is enabled, signUp still emails a confirmation link (→ browser again) and
  won't sign you in until you click it. For a personal app, turn it off: Dashboard → Authentication → Providers → Email
  → disable "Confirm email". Then "Create account" logs you straight in.
